Pizza Bread

Why you'll love it

This meal, is fast, fun, and cheap. Let out your inner child with this easy recipe.

There is something about making pizza bread that brings me back to my childhood days of cheap and easy meals. Except instead of tostinos it's a little fancier. Feel free to deck out your pizza bread however you want, and make it your own. Change up the ingrediants, use homemade pizza sauce, or even get the premade garlic bread from grocery store!

Ingrediants

  • 1 French bread
  • 1 Jar pizza sauce
  • 12oz mozzarella cheese
  • pepperonis

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400.
  2. Cut the french bread in laterally.
  3. Slap that sauce on there (I like a lot!)(can also sprinkly some garlic salt in here.).
  4. Slap that cheese on there (I like a lot of this too!).
  5. Place in oven for 12-15 minutes.
